## 2. Categorical Design
We have split the design controls into logical categories so you can be precise with your typography.

### Available Categories:
- **Site Title & Tagline:** Branding area at the top.
- **Navigation & Sub-menus:** All header links.
- **Blog / Post Titles:** Titles on the blog index and single posts.
- **Content Headers (H1-H6):** Headers *inside* your article content.
- **Body Text:** The standard paragraph text across the site.
- **Sidebars & Footer:** Content within the widget areas.

### What you can customize per category:
- **Font Family & Size:** (Includes built-in Google Fonts and Custom Uploads).
- **Colors:** Independent text and link colors.
- **Effects:** High-end **Drop Shadows** and **Glows** with individual color control.
- **Underlining:** Granular toggles for link decoration.

---

## 3. Background Layers
Aspiring Knight uses a layered background system:
1. **Global Site Background:** The base layer (set a color or upload an image).
2. **Main Wrapper:** This area is permanently transparent to ensure your Global BG is always visible.
3. **Article Box Background:** The specific background for the "content box" behind your text.

---

## 4. Advanced Features
- **Drop Caps:** Enable a dramatic first letter for your posts in **Body Text & Links**.
- **Header Layouts:** Choose between Left, Center, or Right branding in **Header & Branding Assets**.
- **Footer Columns:** Set 1 to 4 widget columns in **Footer Layout**.
- **Top Bar:** Toggle an info strip above the header for announcements or contact info.

---

## 5. Developer Info
- **Tailwind CSS:** This theme is built with Tailwind. All Customizer settings are bridged via CSS variables (`--ak-` prefix).
- **No Build Required:** The theme is shipped with a pre-compiled `assets/css/dist/main.css`. You can use it immediately without running `npm`.
